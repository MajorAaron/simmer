FAQ

Is Simmer a medical device or diagnostic tool?

No. Simmer is informational only — it surfaces patterns in your own logs. It's not a substitute for your physician, and we never recommend medications or diagnoses.

How is this different from mySymptoms or Cara Care?

Those apps log. Simmer interprets. We use a language model to read your diary back to you — finding food, timing, and portion patterns you can't easily see in raw rows.

What about my privacy?

Your entries stay associated with your account, encrypted at rest, and we don't sell or share data with advertisers. Quiet by design.

Can I import my existing Excel sheet?

Yes — paste it directly into the free Trigger Finder. The AI parses free text and ranks triggers immediately.

How does the doctor PDF work?

One-page summary: top 5 triggers, severity timeline, frequency, and your 5 most common foods. Designed to fit on a clipboard.
