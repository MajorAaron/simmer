How Simmer reads your diary

  1. You paste meals & symptoms. Free text or structured rows — whatever's faster.
  2. We parse and weigh. Foods get scored by how often they show up before symptoms. Timing and portion size factor in.
  3. You get plain language. Not a chart of p-values — sentences like "Tomatoes correlate with 8 of your 10 worst nights, especially after 7pm."
  4. Bring it to your GI. One-page summary in the paid tier. Free tier shows the same insights on screen.

Informational only. Simmer is not a diagnostic tool and does not provide medical advice. Always discuss patterns and treatment with your physician.
